Answer 1

In my previous project, I had to develop a login system for sellers while handling multiple security concerns. The deadline was tight, and there were unexpected issues with database integration. I handled it by breaking the project into smaller tasks, prioritizing security, and collaborating with my team for troubleshooting. I also researched best practices for securing login systems, which helped me complete the project successfully.

Weaknesses: Sometimes, I spend too much time perfecting small details. However, I’ve learned to balance quality with efficiency by setting time limits for tasks.
